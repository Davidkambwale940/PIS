Who We Are

PIS Security Services is a Namibian-owned professional security company providing high-quality guarding, patrol, and protection services. Established with a strong commitment to safety and service excellence, we operate in full compliance with Namibian security regulations and industry best practices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make every location under our responsibility as safe and secure as possible for the communities, businesses, and individuals who trust us with their daily safety. We deliver dependable security services through disciplined personnel, proactive risk management, and rapid response while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and ethical conduct.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted Namibian security services provider recognised for integrity, reliability, and service excellence, creating safer environments through continuous improvement and client-focused solutions.
